What we do

Healthy Veg Meal

We provide tiffin services for your mental and physical well-being by making sure you get all the nutrients necessary. A proper combination of Whole grains, Proteins, Fibre, Probiotic along with adequate spices so as there is no compromise on your taste buds.

Healthy Non-veg Meal

We are also delivery non veg dishes on Wednesday & Friday to all those chicken lovers but keeping in mind about other meals we will be having separate kitchen setup to cook non veg food.

Healthy Jain Meal

Keeping in mind the sentiment of our Jain customers, we provide special tiffin services for them. The whole menu is designed excluding potato, Onion, Garlic, Beetroot etc

Healthy Diabetic

Taking advantage of our nutritionist, we have designed a special menu for all the diabetics so they don't have to compromise on their desires. We make sure the diabetic tiffin includes dishes that a diabetic should eat so as to maintain his blood sugar level and also have a variety in his taste.

Healthy Fasting Meal

Fasting has been practiced for thousands of years and is an important tradition in many religions and cultures worldwide. The best healthy food delivery services save you time and help you cook up healthy fasting meals like a pro.

Healthy Diet Meal

Reach your weight, health and workout goals with deliciously healthy diet food. We also have various diet plans customised to suit your specific health and weight requirement. We also take order for events and special occasion. Customer can subscribe our site and also get specials offers.
